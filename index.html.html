Season 01 - Official Rules & Guidelines

Registration Rules

Registration Fees

Type Fee
Player Registration ₹200
Team Owner Registration ₹3000

Icon Player Rule

  • Every team must participate in the auction with one designated Icon Player
  • The Icon Player must be a Faculty member
  • The Icon Player will guide the team during the auction
  • Faculty members who are not directly associated with any team can participate in the auction by registering separately

Team Squad Rules

Squad Size

  • Minimum Squad: 14 players
  • Maximum Squad: 16 players
  • Faculty Players: Minimum 1, Maximum 5 (max 2 in Playing 11)

Faculty Members

  • Team management must inform the organizers in advance about which faculty members will be part of the team
  • Teams can include up to 5 faculty members in their squad
  • A maximum of only 2 faculty members can play in the Playing 11 for any match

Important Information

Team management must inform the organizers in advance about which faculty members will be part of the team.

Playing 11 Composition

Mandatory Composition

  • Faculty: Minimum 01
  • Final Year / B.Tech 3rd Year / Polytechnic: Minimum 02
  • 2nd Year: Minimum 02
  • 1st Year: Minimum 02
  • ITI: Minimum 02

Match Sheet Details

  • Serial Number
  • Player Name
  • Playing Role (Batsman/Bowler/All-rounder/Wicket-Keeper)
  • Year (Academic Year)
  • Branch (Department)
  • Roll Number
  • Verification by Coach
  • Captain's Signature

Impact Players Rule

  • Teams can select 01 Impact Player

Match Rules

Match Format

  • Overs per Match: 8 overs
  • Maximum Bowlers: 4 (A bowler can bowl maximum 2 overs)
  • Bowling Style: Underarm (bowler's hand must rotate) or Round Arm

Powerplay Rules

  • Total Powerplay: 3 overs
  • First 2 overs: Compulsory powerplay
  • Batting team: Can choose 1 over of powerplay between 6th and 8th over

General Rules

  • Umpire's decision: Will be final
  • Leg Byes: Will not be awarded
  • LBW (Leg Before Wicket): Dismissals will not be given
  • All other standard rules of cricket will remain in effect

Trials Rules

  • Every batsman will get 2 overs to bat
  • Every bowler will get 2 overs to bowl
  • Every all-rounder will get 1 over to bat and 1 over to bowl

Auction Rules

Bidding Rules

  • Minimum Base Price: ₹300
  • Bidding will start from the base price
  • From ₹300 to ₹700: Each bid must increase by ₹50
  • Above ₹700: Each bid must increase by ₹100

Player Rules

  • Once a player is declared "SOLD", they cannot be replaced
  • Unsold players will be re-auctioned

League Format

Tournament Structure

  • The league will consist of a total of 32 matches
  • Table Topper (1st place): Will qualify directly for the Final
  • Teams in 2nd and 3rd position: Will play a Qualifier match
  • Grand Final: Winner of Qualifier and Table Topper will play in the Grand Final
